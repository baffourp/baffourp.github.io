About

I am a PhD researcher in theoretical and computational chemistry focusing on excited-state electronic structure, photophysics, and photochemistry of functional molecular systems. My work uses first-principles electronic structure methods and ab initio molecular dynamics to study photoredox catalysts, ionic liquid electrospray thrusters, and reaction mechanisms.

Research Interests

  • Photoredox catalysis and excited-state dynamics
  • Ab initio molecular dynamics
  • Ionic liquid electrospray thrusters
  • Metalloporphyrins and nucleotide dynamics
