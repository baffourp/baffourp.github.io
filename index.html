About

I am a PhD researcher working on photophysics and photochemistry of molecular systems, and with interests in the dynamics of molecules under strong electric fields.

Research Interests

  • Photoredox catalysis and excited-state dynamics
  • Ab initio molecular dynamics
  • Ionic liquid electrospray thrusters
  • Metalloporphyrins and nucleotide dynamics
