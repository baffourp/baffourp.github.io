Research Summary

My research focuses on theoretical and computational chemistry, with emphasis on excited-state electronic structure, photophysics, and photochemistry of functional molecular systems. I employ first-principles electronic structure methods and ab initio molecular dynamics to investigate photoredox catalysts, ionic-liquid ion-source propellants for electrospray thrusters, and reaction mechanisms in complex molecular systems.

Research Interests

  • Photoredox catalysis and excited-state dynamics
  • Ab initio molecular dynamics
  • Ionic liquid electrospray thrusters
  • Metalloporphyrins and nucleotide dynamics
